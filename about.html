Meet Jamie

An individual built from equal parts creative and technical. In addition to completing UCLA's Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp in the Fall of '22, Jamie also spent several years at The Second City in Chicago where he learned the invaluable skills of:

  • Team Building
  • Active Listening - being present and reactive to what's around you
  • Cooperative Creation - the core of the "yes, and" philosophy in improvisation

Jamie also has 20 years of experience in Film, TV, and Stage Production, combined with 25 years in the Service Industry. Some of the skills gained in that time include:

  • Film editing and SFX creation (Adobe, Final Cut)
  • Directing (team leading)
  • Producing (short films and staged plays)
  • Empathy and Insight (from both acting and years as a restaurant server)

As an individual who thrives on discovery, Jamie has found a seemingly endless well in the world of Coding. He hopes that his journey can coincide with yours in a relationship that will be mutually beneficial.
